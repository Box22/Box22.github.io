Hardware Manuals

Who is this list for?

This list is for users who want to know more about the product or who are interested in repairing or replacing parts of their existing puzzlebox. It is also for hardware developers and manufactuers to understand what parts go into a puzzlebox and how to best recreate it for a mass market solution.

Button Face

Part List

1x 4x4 Array of Buttons

1x Arduino Nano

What it's for

This face is designed for human input to the box core for specific puzzles that may require the use of such an array.


LCD Face

Part List

1x LCD Display

1x Arduino Nano

What it's for

This face is for human output as a part of puzzles, possibly for hints or as a part of the puzzle depending on how it's used. Should be able to have each led of the display be able to light up.


Knob Face

Part List

1x LCD Display

4x Knob Potentiometers

1x Arduino Nano

What it's for

This face should be a 2x2 square array of knob potentiometers for twisting control input from the user.


Switch Face

Part List

1x 3x3 Array of switches

1x Arduino Nano

What it's for

This face consists of a 3x3 Array of switches to get human input. Can be used for simple puzzles, binary numbers, etc.


7-Segment and Joystick Face

Part List

4x 7-Segment Display

1x Joystick

1x Arduino Nano

What it's for

This side has a 7-segment display to display numerical values as well as a joystick for interesting inputs. This face could be used for many different applications of a puzzle.


LED Light Face

Part List

4x Red LED

5x Red-Green LED

What it's for

This side has 4 red LEDs and 5 red-green LEDs arranged in a square, the red-green LEDs making a plus shape and the red LEDs filling in the corners. This is used to signal things to the user.
